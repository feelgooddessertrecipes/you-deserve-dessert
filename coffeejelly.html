Certainly! Coffee jelly is a delightful and refreshing dessert.

Ingredients

For the Coffee Jelly

  • 2 cups strong brewed coffee (you can use instant coffee or freshly brewed)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unflavored gelatin
  • 1/4 cup cold water

For the Syrup

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

For Serving

  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Sweetened condensed milk (optional)
  • Ice cream (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Coffee Jelly

  • In a bowl, sprinkle gelatin over cold water and let it sit for a few minutes to bloom.
  • In a saucepan, combine the brewed coffee and sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat until the sugar dissolves, but do not bring it to a boil.
  • Add the bloomed gelatin to the coffee mixture and stir until completely dissolved.
  • Remove the saucepan from heat and let the mixture cool slightly.

2. Pour and Set

  • Pour the coffee mixture into a mold or individual serving glasses.
  • Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.

3. Prepare the Syrup

  • In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water to make the syrup.
  • Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Remove from heat and let it cool.

4. Cut and Serve

  • Once the coffee jelly has set, cut it into cubes or desired shapes.
  • Place the coffee jelly cubes in serving dishes.

5. Serve

  • Drizzle the syrup over the coffee jelly cubes.
  • Optionally, top with whipped cream, sweetened condensed milk, or a scoop of ice cream.
