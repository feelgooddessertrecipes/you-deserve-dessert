Certainly! Strawberry ice cream is a classic favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust based on sweetness preference and the sweetness of strawberries)
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

1.Prepare the Strawberries

  • Wash, hull, and halve the fresh strawberries.

2.Make Strawberry Puree

  • Place the strawberries in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth to make strawberry puree.

3.Mix the Ingredients

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the strawberry puree, granulated sugar, whole milk, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  • Stir the mixture until the sugar is completely dissolved.

4.Chill the Mixture

  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 3-4 hours or overnight. Chilling enhances the flavor and texture.

5.Churn the Ice Cream

  • Once the mixture is well-chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker.
  • Churn the mixture according to your ice cream maker's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

6.Transfer to a Container

  • Transfer the churned strawberry ice cream into a lidded container, spreading it evenly.

7.Freeze

  • Cover the container with a lid and freeze the strawberry ice cream for at least 4-6 hours or until firm.

8.Serve

  • Scoop out the strawberry ice cream and enjoy!
