Certainly! Making watermelon sorbet is a refreshing and simple process.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of seedless watermelon, diced
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar (adjust according to your sweetness preference)
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lime or lemon juice
  • Optional: Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

1.Prepare the Watermelon

  • Remove the seeds from the watermelon and cut it into small chunks.

2.Freeze the Watermelon

  • Place the watermelon chunks on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  • Freeze the watermelon chunks for at least 2-3 hours, or until they are solid.

3.Make the Sorbet

  • Once the watermelon chunks are frozen, transfer them to a blender or food processor.
  • Add the granulated sugar and lime or lemon juice to the frozen watermelon.

4.Blend

  • Blend the ingredients until smooth. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the blender or food processor a few times.

5.Taste and Adjust

  • Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness or acidity if needed. Add more sugar or lime/lemon juice according to your preference.

6.Serve

  • Once you achieve a smooth consistency, transfer the sorbet mixture to a container and freeze for an additional 2-4 hours to firm up.

7.Serve and Garnish

  • Scoop the sorbet into bowls or cones and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
