Ingredients

For the Brownie Layer

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Caramel Layer

  • 1 cup caramel candies (unwrapped) or caramel sauce
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  6. In a small saucepan, melt the caramels with the heavy cream over low heat, stirring continuously until smooth. If you're using caramel sauce, just warm it up.
  7. Pour the caramel mixture evenly over the first layer of brownie batter.
  8. Carefully spoon the remaining brownie batter over the caramel layer and spread it to cover.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  10. Allow the caramel brownies to cool completely before cutting them into squares.
