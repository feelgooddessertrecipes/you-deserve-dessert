Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4-5 chocolate bars of your choice (milk chocolate, dark chocolate, or a combination)
  • Optional: Chopped nuts, sprinkles, or other toppings

Instructions

1.Prepare the Chocolate Bars

  • Unwrap the chocolate bars and chop them into small, bite-sized pieces. You can use a knife or a food processor for this step.

2.Prepare the Ice Cream Base

  • n a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.

3.Combine Ingredients

  • Add the chopped chocolate bars into the ice cream base mixture. Stir well to evenly distribute the chocolate pieces throughout the mixture.

4.Chill the Mixture

  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2-3 hours, or preferably overnight. This allows the flavors to meld and the mixture to chill thoroughly.

5.Churn the Ice Cream

  • Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.

6.Add Toppings (Optional)

  • If desired, add chopped nuts, sprinkles, or any other toppings during the last few minutes of churning.

7.Transfer and Freeze

  • Transfer the churned ice cream into a lidded container. Smooth the top with a spatula, cover with the lid, and freeze for at least 4-6 hours, or until firm.

8.Serve and Enjoy

  • Once the chocolate bar ice cream is fully frozen, scoop it into bowls or cones and enjoy your homemade treat!
