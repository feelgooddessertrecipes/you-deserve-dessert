Ingredients

For Jalebi

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of orange food color (optional)
  • Ghee or oil for frying

For Sugar Syrup

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • A few saffron strands
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, gram flour, yogurt, baking soda, and food color (if using). Add water as needed to make a smooth batter. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate pan, combine sugar and water for the syrup. Bring it to a boil, stirring until the sugar dissolves. Add saffron strands, cardamom powder, and lemon juice. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until it reaches a one-string consistency. Keep the syrup warm.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Pour the batter into a squeeze bottle or a piping bag with a small tip.
  5. Squeeze the batter into hot oil in a spiral or pretzel shape. Fry until the jalebis are golden and crisp. Remove excess oil by placing them on absorbent paper.
  6. Dip the fried jalebis into the warm sugar syrup, ensuring they are well-coated. Let them soak for a few minutes.
  7. Serve the jalebis warm and enjoy the sweet, syrupy goodness!
