Ingredients

  • 1 box of your favorite chocolate cake mix (and the ingredients needed to prepare it)
  • 1.5 quarts of your favorite ice cream (vanilla, chocolate, or a combination)
  • 2 cups of whipped cream
  • Chocolate sauce or fudge for drizzling (optional)
  • Your choice of toppings (sprinkles, crushed cookies, chopped nuts, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven and prepare the chocolate cake batter according to the instructions on the box.
  2. Grease and flour two round cake pans. Divide the batter evenly between the pans.
  3. Bake the cakes according to the package instructions. Once baked, let them cool completely.
  4. Take the ice cream out of the freezer and let it soften for a few minutes.
  5. Place one of the cooled chocolate cakes on a serving plate. Spread a layer of softened ice cream on top of the cake.
  6. Place the second chocolate cake on top of the ice cream layer. Press down gently to secure the layers.
  7. Frost the entire cake with whipped cream, covering it completely.
  8. If desired, drizzle chocolate sauce or fudge over the top of the cake.
  9. Decorate the cake with your favorite toppings, such as sprinkles, crushed cookies, or chopped nuts.
  10. Place the ice cream cake in the freezer for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow it to set.
