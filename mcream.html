Certainly! Making mango ice cream at home is a delightful and simple process.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ripe mango puree (about 2-3 large mangoes)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar (adjust according to your sweetness preference)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

1.Prepare the Mango Puree

  • Peel and dice the ripe mangoes.
  • Place the mango pieces in a blender or food processor and blend until smooth to make mango puree.

2.Mix the Ingredients

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the mango puree, heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract (if using), and a pinch of salt.
  • Stir the mixture until the sugar is completely dissolved.

3.Chill the Mixture

  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 3-4 hours, or preferably overnight. Chilling helps to enhance the flavor and texture.

4.Churn the Ice Cream

  • Once the mixture is well-chilled, pour it into an ice cream maker.
  • Churn the mixture according to your ice cream maker's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

5.Transfer to a Container

  • Transfer the churned ice cream into a lidded container, spreading it evenly.

6.Freeze

  • Cover the container with a lid and freeze the mango ice cream for at least 4-6 hours or until firm.

7.Serve

  • Scoop out the mango ice cream and serve it in bowls or cones.
