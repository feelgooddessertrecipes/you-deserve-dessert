Ingredients

For the crust

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

For the filling

  • 1 can (15 ounces) pure pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup evaporated milk (or half-and-half)

Instructions

1.For the crust

  • In a food processor, combine the flour, sugar, and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  • Gradually add the ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and pulse until the dough comes together. Be careful not to overmix.
  • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, gather it into a ball, and then flatten it into a disc. Wrap it in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

2.For the filling

  • Preheat your oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C).
  • In a large bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt until well combined.
  • Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  • Gradually add the evaporated milk, stirring until smooth.

3.Assembling the pie

  • Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the rolled-out dough in the pie dish, trimming any excess hanging over the edges.
  • Pour the pumpkin filling into the pie crust.

4.Baking

  • Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  • Reduce the oven temperature to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and continue baking for an additional 40-50 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Allow the pie to cool completely before serving.
