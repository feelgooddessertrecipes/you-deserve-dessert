Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold

For the Chocolate Filling

  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Assembly

  • Flour for dusting
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

1.Prepare the Dough

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  • Cut the cold butter into small cubes and incorporate it into the flour mixture until you have a crumbly texture.
  • Gradually add cold water, stirring until the dough comes together. Form it into a rectangle, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

2.Make the Butter Layer

  • On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a larger rectangle.
  • Place the butter in the center of the dough and fold the sides over the butter, sealing the edges.
  • Roll out the dough with the enclosed butter into a rectangle again, then fold it in thirds like a letter. This is one fold.
  • Refrigerate for 30 minutes. Repeat this folding process (rolling and folding) two more times, chilling between each fold.

3. Prepare the Chocolate Filling

  • In a bowl, mix together the finely chopped chocolate, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, softened butter, and vanilla extract until smooth.

4.Assemble the Chocolate Puff Pastry

  • Preheat your oven to 400Â°F (200Â°C).
  • On a floured surface, roll out the dough into a large rectangle.
  • Spread the chocolate filling evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Roll the dough tightly from one of the longer sides, creating a log.
  • Cut the log into slices, about 1 inch thick.
  • Place the slices on a parchment-lined baking sheet, leaving space between each.

5. Bake

  • Beat an egg and brush it over the tops of the pastry slices for a golden finish.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the chocolate puff pastry is golden brown and puffed up.
  • Allow it to cool slightly before serving.
