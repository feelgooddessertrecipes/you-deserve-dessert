Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan, combine 3 1/2 cups of milk and sugar. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sugar dissolves. Be careful not to bring it to a boil.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the remaining 1/2 cup of milk and cornstarch until well combined and smooth.
  3. Once the milk and sugar mixture is warm, gradually whisk in the cornstarch mixture. Continue to whisk constantly to avoid any lumps from forming.
  4. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens and comes to a gentle boil. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, continuing to stir.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  7. Pour the pudding into individual serving dishes or one large bowl.
  8. Allow the pudding to cool to room temperature before placing it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 2 hours or until set.
  9. Once chilled, you can serve the milk pudding on its own or with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, whipped cream, or a sprinkle of cinnamon.
