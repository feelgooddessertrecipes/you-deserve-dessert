Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Green food coloring
  • Assorted gem candies
  • Icing for decoration (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the egg and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  5. Add green food coloring to the dough until you achieve your desired shade of green.
  6. Divide the dough in half and shape each half into a disk. Wrap them in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  7. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  8. Roll out one disk of dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use a Christmas tree cookie cutter to cut out tree shapes.
  9. Place the cutouts on the prepared baking sheets and press gem candies onto each tree to decorate.
  10. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Optional: Use icing to add additional decorations or create patterns on the trees.
