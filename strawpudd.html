Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

1.Prepare Strawberries

  • Wash and hull the strawberries. Chop them into small pieces.

2.Make Strawberry Puree

  • In a blender or food processor, puree the strawberries until smooth. Set aside.

3.Mix Dry Ingredients

  • In a medium saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.

4.Combine Wet Ingredients

  • Gradually whisk in the milk until the mixture is smooth.

5.Cook on Stovetop

  • Place the saucepan over medium heat and cook the mixture, stirring constantly, until it thickens and comes to a boil.

6.Temper Egg Yolks

  • In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1 cup of the hot milk mixture to temper the eggs. Then, add the egg mixture back to the saucepan.

7.Cook Until Thickened

  • Continue to cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens.

8.Add Strawberry Puree

  • Stir in the strawberry puree and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until well combined.

9.Add Butter and Vanilla

  • Remove the saucepan from heat. Stir in the butter and vanilla extract until the butter is melted and the mixture is smooth.

10.Cool and Serve

  • Allow the pudding to cool slightly before transferring it to serving dishes or a large bowl. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.

11.Garnish (Optional)

  • Garnish with additional sliced strawberries or a dollop of whipped cream before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade strawberry pudding!😋
