Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 1 cup warm milk (110Â°F/45Â°C)
  • 2 packages (1/4 ounce each) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs

For the Filling

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the Icing

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Purple, green, and gold colored sugar or sprinkles (traditional Mardi Gras colors)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the sugar and softened butter until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Add the activated yeast mixture to the bowl and mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
  7. While the dough is rising, prepare the filling by mixing together the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Once the dough has risen, punch it down, and roll it out on a floured surface into a rectangle (approximately 10x20 inches).
  9. Brush the melted butter over the dough, then sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly.
  10. Roll the dough tightly from the longer side, forming a log. Place the log on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, seam side down, and shape it into an oval or ring.
  11. Allow the shaped dough to rise for another 30-45 minutes.
  12. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Bake the king cake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  13. While the king cake is cooling, prepare the icing by mixing powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth. Drizzle the icing over the cooled cake.
  14. Sprinkle colored sugar or sprinkles over the icing in alternating sections of purple, green, and gold, representing the traditional Mardi Gras colors.
