TIRUNELVELI HALWA
Tamil desserts not only satisfy your sweet tooth but also bring a host of benefits. Many of them are made with ingredients like jaggery, coconut, and various grains, offering nutritional goodness along with deliciousness. For example, jaggery is a healthier alternative to refined sugar, packed with minerals like iron and vitamins. Coconut, a staple in Tamil desserts, provides healthy fats and is rich in fiber.
