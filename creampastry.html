Ingredients

For the Choux Pastry

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

For the Filling

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Ganache (Optional)

  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Instructions

1.Preheat the Oven

  • Preheat your oven to 425Â°F (220Â°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2.Make the Choux Pastry

  • In a saucepan, combine water, butter, and salt. Bring it to a boil over medium heat.
  • Remove the saucepan from heat and quickly stir in the flour until the mixture forms a ball.
  • Allow the mixture to cool for a couple of minutes.
  • Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, until the batter is smooth.

3.Pipe and Bake

  • Transfer the choux pastry dough to a piping bag fitted with a large round tip.
  • Pipe small mounds onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the puffs are golden brown and puffed up.

4.Prepare the Filling

  • In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

5.Fill the Puffs

  • Once the cream puffs are completely cooled, cut them in half horizontally.
  • Fill the bottom halves with the whipped cream using a spoon or piping bag.

6.Optional Chocolate Ganache

  • If desired, make a chocolate ganache by heating the heavy cream until it's almost boiling and pouring it over the chocolate chips. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until smooth.
  • Dip the filled cream puff tops into the chocolate ganache or drizzle it over the filled puffs.

7.Serve and Enjoy

  • Place the chocolate-covered tops onto the cream-filled bottoms.
  • Dust with powdered sugar if desired and serve.
